OUR SERVICES

Taxi, Minibus and Wheelchair Accessible General Hire

Taxi and minibus hire is available on request or pre-booked for general travel, airport runs or bespoke functions such as weddings or special events.

Passenger Assistant

Escort service for children and the vulnerable to and from schools, colleges, etc.  All staff are DBS vetted and some staff are enhanced passenger assistants are available for specialist needs such as sign language and medically trained escorts.

Courier service for NHS trusts

We provide immediate courier services for NHS trusts in Havering and surrounding London boroughs where immediate transportation is required.   Proud to have been supporting the NHS for over 30 years.
